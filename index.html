What is ENSO? Measured in the equatorial Pacific Ocean (Niño 3.4 region). Its effects reach Indonesia via the Walker Circulation — when the Pacific shifts, Indonesia’s rainfall regime shifts too. ONI above +0.5°C = El Niño; below -0.5°C = La Niña.
